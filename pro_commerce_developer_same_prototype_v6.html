About Us

Your trusted partner for Square payment processing and POS guidance

Pro Commerce Solutions exists to make payment processing easier for small businesses that want a clear Square setup, practical hardware guidance, and a reliable onboarding path.

Empowering small businesses with smarter payment tools

Welcome to Pro Commerce Solutions, your trusted partner in modern payment processing. The business is focused on helping small businesses simplify how they accept payments, manage transactions, and prepare for long-term growth using Square-powered solutions.

Pro Commerce Solutions understands that every business operates differently. A dine-in restaurant may need kitchen coordination and table-service tools. A fast-service concept may need speed, kiosks, and efficient checkout. A retail store may need strong front-counter flows, customer payments, and hardware that fits daily operations.

That is why the website should position Dominique as a practical advisor—not just a salesperson. The message should be that Pro Commerce Solutions helps business owners understand their options, choose the right setup, and move through onboarding with clarity and confidence.

05 / Forms + Chatbot

Lead collection experience

These are the key user inputs the live website should capture through the optional survey, upload flow, and chatbot.

Survey form

The optional survey appears when the visitor lands on the website or when they click a recommendation CTA. It should capture:

  • First name, last name, email address, phone number
  • Industry, monthly revenue, projected monthly volume
  • Business type: restaurant (table service), restaurant (quick service), retail, other
  • Merchant DBA, legal business name, street address, state, city, zip
  • Recent processing statement upload

AI chatbot

The chatbot should answer simple questions, guide visitors to plans or hardware, and capture lead details if the visitor is ready to talk.

  • Quick questions: “I need a Square POS system”, “I want hardware pricing”, “I run a restaurant”, “I want to upload a statement”
  • Lead source saved as Chatbot in Google Sheets
  • Transcript or summary saved in internal notes
